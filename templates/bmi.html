About BMI Calculator

BMI (Body Mass Index) is a measure that uses your height and weight to work out if your weight is healthy. It is calculated by dividing your weight in kilograms by the square of your height in meters.

Use this calculator to determine your BMI. Simply enter your weight in kilograms and your height in centimeters, and click "Calculate BMI" to see your result.

Remember, BMI is a useful indicator of overall fitness for most people, but it may not be accurate for everyone, including athletes and the elderly.
